# v2.1.1 Public Polish Status

Status: implemented.

Changes:

- Updated public version strings to `v2.1.1-public-polish`.
- Made `--target-zn-si` and `--target-zn-count` mutually exclusive in the CLI.
- Preserved the default Zn target behavior: if neither option is supplied, `--target-zn-si 0.05` is used.
- Clarified site-mode meanings in public documentation, especially single-Zn versus multi-Zn modes.
- Clarified that optional `--run-quasistatic` performs plus/minus small-strain x-direction diagnostic input checks only.
- Added post-minimization CS-Info reattachment and post-min validation when `--run-static-relaxation` is used.
- Documented the role of the custom `CS-Info` section and the limited purpose of `.clean.data` exports.

Boundaries preserved:

- CementFF4-Zn force-field parameters were not changed.
- Validation semantics were not changed.
- The 2.5 A Zn-O coordination threshold was not relaxed.
- The old `mixed_Q1_Q2b_Zn` site type was not enabled.
- Finite-temperature MD was not introduced.
- No `md_ready_candidate` label is introduced.
- No final elastic constants or production mechanical properties are claimed.
- Q1 mechanics and multi-Zn mechanics remain opt-in.
